How we work

FlairMyTrip Web Portal focuses on B2B clients, offering functions to streamline visa processing. The eVisa web portal simplifies the visa application process for B2B clients, providing a convenient platform for managing visa applications for their customers. The process includes the following steps:

Register with Us

After registration, clients undergo KYC and sign a Service Agreement.

Submit & Pay

Submit customer and travel details, upload documents, confirm, and make payment.

Wait For Approval

Our team efficiently handles applications, liaises with authorities, and monitors progress

Get Visa

On Approval visas will be posted on the portal, with notifications emailed.
